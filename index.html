Overtime Submission Guidelines

  1. Fill in your details in the Submitter Information section
  2. Add overtime entries using the "Add New Entry" button
  3. Required fields: Employee Code, Name, Project Code, Date, and Hours
  4. Format:
    • Employee Code: E5704
    • Project Code: LS00001, GC00001, DM00001, CN00001 or 200101
    • Hours: 0.5 to 12 hours in 0.5 hour increments
  5. Add description of the overtime activity performed
  6. Submit for HR review and payroll processing
Note: All overtime submissions require supervisor approval. False information may result in disciplinary action.
